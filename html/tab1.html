Composition of the universe

The universe is made up of 66.2 percent dark energy and the remaining 33.8 percent is a combination of ordinary matter - planets or stars that we see glowing - and dark matter, according to a new analysis that refines the nature of the cosmos. and opens the door to more exact observations.
